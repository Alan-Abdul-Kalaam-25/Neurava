Family Resources

Find information to help you support children who are struggling with mental health, behavior or learning challenges.

Published 15.07.2020 by Anna

As a parent, it can be difficult to navigate the complex world of mental health and wellness for your child. At Your_helping-hand, we understand these challenges and are here to provide you with the resources and support you need to help your child thrive.

What all type of resources we host?

Articles and blog posts: Our website features a range of articles and blog posts on various mental health topics, including common mental health conditions, treatment options, and tips for promoting mental wellness in your child.

Webinars and workshops: We offer educational webinars and workshops led by mental health professionals, covering topics such as anxiety, depression, and ADHD.

Support groups: We provide access to online support groups for parents of children with mental health conditions, allowing you to connect with others who may be going through similar experiences.

Referral services: If you need help finding a mental health provider for your child, we offer referral services to connect you with qualified professionals in your area.
