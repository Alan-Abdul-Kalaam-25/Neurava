About Neurava

Our platform is dedicated to transforming the lives of children and families struggling with mental health and learning disorders by giving them the help they need to thrive.

We're the leading independent nonprofit in children's mental health, operating three Mission Areas that work together for greater impact: Care, Education and Science.

Our Mission: We aim at sharpening mental skills of children through various games which help them learn patterns, sentences and develop higher order thinking skills with an interactive platform.

Parent's Role

Parents play an important role in the mental health development of their child.

Build a positive and supportive relationship with your child: A positive and supportive relationship can help them feel safe, secure, and loved. This can build their self-esteem and resilience, and help them develop healthy coping skills.
