Donate Mental Skills Home

Description

The video provides practical tips and strategies that parents and caregivers can use to help children and teenagers manage their stress.
These include encouraging open communication, promoting healthy lifestyle habits like exercise and sleep,
and seeking professional help when necessary. The narrator uses clear and empathetic language, accompanied by relatable
examples and anecdotes, to make the topic of stress in children and teenagers accessible and easy
to understand for viewers of all ages.
